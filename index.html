Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew Nuts
  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Water
  • Cardamom Powder

Recipe

  1. Grind the cashew nuts to a fine powder.
  2. Heat sugar and water in a pan until it reaches consistency.
  3. Add the cashew powder and milk powder to the sugar syrup.
  4. Spread the mixture on a greased plate and let it cool.
  5. Cut into diamond shapes and serve.

Pizza

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

Recipe

  1. Mix flour, yeast, salt and water to make a dough.
  2. Let the dough rise for 2 hours.
  3. Roll out the dough and spread tomato sauce on it.
  4. Add cheese and toppings of your choice.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 200 degrees for 20 minutes.
